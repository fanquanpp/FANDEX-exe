## 1. 商务邮件基本格式

### 1.1 邮件结构

```
Subject Line (主题行)
    ↓
Salutation (称呼)
    ↓
Opening (开场白)
    ↓
Body (正文)
    ↓
Closing (结尾)
    ↓
Sign-off (落款)
    ↓
Signature (签名)
```

### 1.2 各部分详解

**Subject Line（主题行）：**

| 原则   | 说明           | 示例                               |
| ------ | -------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| 简洁   | 不超过 50 字符 | Meeting Rescheduled to June 15     |
| 具体   | 明确邮件内容   | Q3 Budget Review - Action Required |
| 可搜索 | 包含关键词     | Project Alpha - Sprint 3 Update    |

**Salutation（称呼）：**

| 场合       | 称呼                     | 示例                    |
| ---------- | ------------------------ | ----------------------- |
| 正式       | Dear + Title + Last Name | Dear Mr. Smith,         |
| 半正式     | Dear + First Name        | Dear John,              |
| 非正式     | Hi + First Name          | Hi Sarah,               |
| 群体       | Dear All / Hi Team       | Dear All,               |
| 未知收件人 | To Whom It May Concern   | To Whom It May Concern, |

**Sign-off（落款）：**

| 正式程度 | 落款                           |
| -------- | ------------------------------ |
| 正式     | Sincerely, / Yours faithfully, |
| 半正式   | Best regards, / Kind regards,  |
| 非正式   | Best, / Cheers, / Thanks,      |

## 2. 常用句式

### 2.1 开场白

| 功能     | 句式                                           |
| -------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| 回复邮件 | Thank you for your email regarding...          |
| 主动联系 | I am writing to inquire about...               |
| 跟进     | I am following up on our conversation about... |
| 介绍自己 | I am writing to introduce myself as...         |
| 转发     | I am forwarding you the email from...          |

### 2.2 提出请求

| 礼貌程度 | 句式                                                              |
| -------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 非常礼貌 | I would appreciate it if you could...                             |
| 礼貌     | Could you please...?                                              |
| 直接     | Please...                                                         |
| 紧急     | I would be grateful if you could... at your earliest convenience. |

### 2.3 附加信息

| 功能 | 句式                                  |
| ---- | ------------------------------------- |
| 附件 | Please find attached...               |
| 链接 | For more information, please visit... |
| 参考 | As discussed in our meeting...        |
| 补充 | I would also like to add that...      |

### 2.4 表达意见

| 功能     | 句式                                                     |
| -------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| 同意     | I agree with your assessment that...                     |
| 部分同意 | I agree with X, however, I have concerns about Y.        |
| 不同意   | I understand your point, but I believe...                |
| 建议     | I would suggest/recommend that...                        |
| 提醒     | Please note that... / I would like to remind you that... |

### 2.5 结尾

| 功能     | 句式                                                            |
| -------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 期待回复 | I look forward to hearing from you.                             |
| 期待会面 | I look forward to meeting you on [date].                        |
| 提供帮助 | Please do not hesitate to contact me if you have any questions. |
| 感谢     | Thank you for your time and consideration.                      |
| 后续行动 | I will follow up with you by [date].                            |

## 3. 场景模板

### 3.1 请求信息 (Requesting Information)

**Subject:** Inquiry Regarding [Topic]

> Dear [Name],
>
> I am writing to inquire about [topic]. Specifically, I would like to know:
>
> 1. [Question 1]
> 2. [Question 2]
> 3. [Question 3]
>
> Could you please provide this information at your earliest convenience? If there is a better contact person for this inquiry, please let me know.
>
> Thank you for your time and assistance.
>
> Best regards,
> [Your Name]

### 3.2 会议邀请 (Meeting Invitation)

**Subject:** Meeting Request: [Topic] - [Date]

> Dear [Name],
>
> I would like to schedule a meeting to discuss [topic]. The proposed details are as follows:
>
> - **Date:** [Date]
> - **Time:** [Time] ([Timezone])
> - **Duration:** [Duration]
> - **Location:** [Location/Link]
> - **Agenda:**
>   1. [Agenda item 1]
>   2. [Agenda item 2]
>   3. [Agenda item 3]
>
> Please let me know if this time works for you, or suggest an alternative.
>
> Best regards,
> [Your Name]

### 3.3 项目更新 (Project Update)

**Subject:** Project [Name] - Weekly Update ([Date])

> Hi Team,
>
> Here is the weekly update for Project [Name]:
>
> **Completed:**
>
> - [Completed item 1]
> - [Completed item 2]
>
> **In Progress:**
>
> - [In-progress item 1] (Expected completion: [Date])
> - [In-progress item 2] (Expected completion: [Date])
>
> **Upcoming:**
>
> - [Upcoming item 1]
> - [Upcoming item 2]
>
> **Blockers:**
>
> - [Blocker description] - [Mitigation plan]
>
> Please let me know if you have any questions or concerns.
>
> Best regards,
> [Your Name]

### 3.4 面试感谢信 (Post-Interview Thank You)

**Subject:** Thank You - [Position] Interview

> Dear [Interviewer's Name],
>
> Thank you for taking the time to speak with me today about the [Position] role at [Company]. I truly enjoyed learning more about the team and the exciting projects you are working on.
>
> Our conversation reinforced my enthusiasm for this opportunity. I am particularly excited about [specific aspect discussed], and I believe my experience in [relevant skill] would enable me to contribute meaningfully to your team.
>
> Please do not hesitate to reach out if you need any additional information. I look forward to hearing from you regarding the next steps.
>
> Best regards,
> [Your Name]

### 3.5 投诉 (Making a Complaint)

**Subject:** Complaint Regarding [Issue] - Order #[Number]

> Dear [Name/Customer Service],
>
> I am writing to express my dissatisfaction with [product/service] that I [purchased/received] on [date].
>
> The issue is as follows: [Describe the problem clearly and factually].
>
> I have already [steps taken to resolve, e.g., contacted support, tried troubleshooting]. However, the issue remains unresolved.
>
> I would appreciate it if you could [desired resolution, e.g., issue a refund, replace the product, schedule a repair] within [timeframe].
>
> Please find attached [relevant documents, e.g., receipt, photos, screenshots].
>
> I look forward to your prompt response.
>
> Sincerely,
> [Your Name]

### 3.6 道歉 (Apology)

**Subject:** Apology Regarding [Issue]

> Dear [Name],
>
> I am writing to sincerely apologize for [issue]. I understand that this has caused [impact], and I take full responsibility for the inconvenience.
>
> The reason for this issue was [brief explanation without making excuses]. We have taken the following steps to resolve it:
>
> 1. [Corrective action 1]
> 2. [Corrective action 2]
>
> To prevent this from happening again, we have [preventive measure].
>
> We value your [business/partnership/relationship] and are committed to providing you with the best possible service. Please let me know if there is anything else we can do.
>
> Sincerely,
> [Your Name]

### 3.7 辞职信 (Resignation)

**Subject:** Resignation - [Your Name]

> Dear [Manager's Name],
>
> I am writing to formally notify you of my resignation from my position as [Position] at [Company], effective [Date].
>
> I am grateful for the opportunities and experiences I have gained during my time here. I have learned a great deal and enjoyed working with the team.
>
> I am committed to ensuring a smooth transition and will complete all pending tasks before my departure. Please let me know how I can best assist during the transition period.
>
> I wish you and the team all the best for the future.
>
> Sincerely,
> [Your Name]

## 4. 邮件写作注意事项

### 4.1 礼貌原则

| 原则         | 说明                                  |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------- |
| 使用礼貌用语 | please, thank you, I would appreciate |
| 避免命令语气 | Could you please... 而非 You must...  |
| 考虑文化差异 | 不同文化的礼貌标准不同                |
| 及时回复     | 24小时内回复商务邮件                  |

### 4.2 清晰原则

| 原则             | 说明                               |
| ---------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| 一封邮件一个主题 | 不要在一封邮件中讨论多个不相关话题 |
| 使用列表         | 多个要点用编号列表                 |
| 突出重点         | 关键信息加粗                       |
| 明确行动         | 明确指出需要对方做什么             |

### 4.3 常见错误

| 错误           | 正确           | 说明                       |
| -------------- | -------------- | -------------------------- |
| Reply All 滥用 | 只回复必要的人 | 避免给所有人发不必要的邮件 |
| 主题为空       | 填写清晰主题   | 主题是邮件的第一印象       |
| 附件遗漏       | 发送前检查附件 | 先添加附件再写正文         |
| 过长邮件       | 控制在一屏内   | 超长邮件附摘要             |
| 情绪化表达     | 冷静后再发送   | 不要在愤怒时发邮件         |
